The Lab

A creative workshop for ideas that started small and refused to stay that way.

Morning Launchpad

What it is: A family dashboard for schedules, weather, and reminders.

Why it exists: Because mornings should feel like teamwork, not emergency mode.

Dad Joke Generator Pro

What it is: A tiny web app that serves painfully excellent jokes on demand.

Why it exists: To keep groans at healthy household levels.

Trip Brain

What it is: A prototype planner that maps stops, snacks, and timing.

Why it exists: Family adventures deserve less friction and more fun.
