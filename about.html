About Me

I’m a dad first, builder second, and occasional overthinker of snack logistics at soccer games.

I love creating things that make people smile — whether that’s a tiny app that solves a silly problem, a family trip plan that actually survives contact with reality, or a story that makes everyone at the dinner table laugh-snort.

Around here, I keep it honest: wins, fails, weird ideas, and little lessons from parenting and building. No polished superhero routine, just a real human trying to make cool stuff and raise kind kids.

What I’m into

  • Family adventures and memory-making missions
  • Designing web apps and creative prototypes
  • Humor, storytelling, and useful chaos
  • Turning “someday ideas” into “launched tonight”

About Me

Dad first. Builder second. Professional finder of missing water bottles. Reluctant owner of 37 unfinished side ideas.

I love family life, storytelling, and building useful things on the web. Some ideas become tools. Some become cautionary tales. Either way, they usually teach me something.

I’m not trying to look perfect here. This is the real version: meaningful moments, failed experiments, small wins, loud laughs, and the occasional “what was I thinking?” episode.

What drives this site

  • Keep family memories alive without turning life into a highlight reel.
  • Share stories honestly, with humor and zero corporate voice.
  • Build creative tools in public and explain why they exist.
  • Stay curious, stay human, and ship things anyway.
