About Me

I’m a dad first, builder second, and occasional overthinker of snack logistics at soccer games.

I love creating things that make people smile — whether that’s a tiny app that solves a silly problem, a family trip plan that actually survives contact with reality, or a story that makes everyone at the dinner table laugh-snort.

Around here, I keep it honest: wins, fails, weird ideas, and little lessons from parenting and building. No polished superhero routine, just a real human trying to make cool stuff and raise kind kids.

What I’m into

  • Family adventures and memory-making missions
  • Designing web apps and creative prototypes
  • Humor, storytelling, and useful chaos
  • Turning “someday ideas” into “launched tonight”
