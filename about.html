The Man Behind the Bad Decisions

ThorDad is a dad, a family-centered human, a creative thinker, and a builder who keeps turning normal observations into projects with names that sound like they need legal review.

Who this is

Family first, ideas second, sleep allegedly on the list.

TD exists because life moves fast, kids say unforgettable things at inconvenient times, and good ideas tend to arrive during dishes, beach drives, or the final ten minutes before bed. This site keeps the meaningful pieces together: family stories, public updates, creative builds, and the occasional note that sounds sarcastic because it is accurate.

Operating style: tell the truth, keep the jokes dry, build useful things, and never underestimate the emotional weight of a quiet beach day.
