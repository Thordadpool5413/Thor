About Me

I’m a dad, husband, creative thinker, and builder with a soft spot for family stories and weirdly ambitious side projects.

I care a lot about the people I love and the things I build. Sometimes those overlap, usually around breakfast when I’m debugging a tiny app while looking for someone’s missing shoe.

I’m not trying to sound polished here. I’m trying to sound real. This site is honest life updates, meaningful moments, wins, misses, and the occasional joke that only parents will truly appreciate.

Who you’ll meet here

  • A family-centered human who cares deeply and laughs often.
  • A builder who turns messy ideas into working prototypes.
  • A storyteller who wants to remember what mattered.
  • A dad with enough self-awareness to know this whole site is absurd and still worth making.
