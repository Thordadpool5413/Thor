        What is a period?

        A period is when blood leaves your body from your vagina. It means your body is growing, and that's normal.

        How long does it last?

        Many periods last about 3 to 7 days. It can be different for each person, and that's okay.

        What can help with cramps?

        Try a warm pad, water, rest, or gentle stretching. Ask a trusted adult if pain feels strong.

        When should I ask for help?

        If you feel very scared, very sad, dizzy, or have very heavy bleeding, talk to a parent, guardian, school nurse, or doctor.

        💖 Reminder: You are not alone. Your feelings matter.
