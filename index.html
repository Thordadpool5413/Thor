What this is

A personal headquarters with sand in the floor mats.

ThorDad is the bigger personality. TD is the shorter name that fits on buttons, folders, and ideas that have not yet earned a dramatic title. Together they hold the public side of family memories, running commentary, creative work, apps, websites, and dad-brain systems that sound unnecessary until they save a Saturday.

Brand rule: cinematic, warm, sharp, and real. No capes, no borrowed costumes, no pretending the laundry folded itself.
