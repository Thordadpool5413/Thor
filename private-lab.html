Memory Sorter Console

A private test project for organizing family memories into albums, story cards, milestones, and year sections.

What it does

Groups memory entries by year, event, and highlight type.

Why it exists

Because the camera roll is not a filing strategy.

How to test

Add sample captions, tag them, and check whether the same memory can be found later without a search party.

Known issues

Needs real authentication and better bulk upload handling.

Future ideas

Movie Night Negotiator

A private prototype for choosing a movie before everyone loses the will to watch one.

What it does

Ranks options by runtime, mood, rating, rewatch tolerance, and snack readiness.

Why it exists

Decision fatigue should not be the villain of movie night.

How to test

Enter three options, pick a mood, and verify whether the winner feels defensible.

Known issues

Still lets people say “I don’t care” while caring deeply.

Future ideas

Streaming availability, family profiles, and a veto cooldown.
